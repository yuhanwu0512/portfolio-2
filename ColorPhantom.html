ColorPhantom is a website game based on a color theory named "Simultaneous Color Contrast Effect". This effect is when two adjacent colors, seen at the same time (“simultaneously”) affect our perception or related performance. As shown in the picture below, the two small rectangles are actually the exact same color, but because of different surroundings, the one with darker surrounding looks much lighter than the one with lighter surrounding.


The game has 10 questions. In each question, players need to pick the same color in the four choices as the pointed pixel. When all the questions are answered, there will be a score and explanations for this color phenomenon.

Some design decisions

The website was built with HTML and we also added a google form which will record people's answers automatically. We received 310 answers in total and wrote a paper with the data.
