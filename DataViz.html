1. The Century of Movies

How is the movie industry developing? With data from 5000+ movies, this graph shows the average IMDb score of each decade from 1916-2016 compared with the overall average score, along with average Facebook likes and average budget. The word clouds show popular movie genres in each decade. We got a lot of interesting findings from this graph. Click here to learn about it!

2. Distribution of Pokemons in Manhattan

This gamified visualization web shows the distribution of Pokemon in Manhattan in the game Pokemon Go and how it is affected by time and weather. You can also search to learn about pokemon here and let them fight!

3. Paper collaboration in Gates Hall

As the title shows, this work shows how the professors in Gates Hall collaborate with each other. Is it true that geographical distance will affect how much professors collaborate? Who is the social queen of Gates Hall? Find it out here!

4. Color Phantom

Is it true that what you see is what it is? Your eyes can always fool you. Play Color Phantom to learn how Simultaneous Color Contrast Effect work!
